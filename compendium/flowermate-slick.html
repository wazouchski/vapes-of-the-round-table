# Flowermate Slick

Overview

The Flowermate Slick is a portable dry herb vaporizer manufactured by Flowermate (Shenzhen Smiss Technology Co.), a Chinese company known for producing a range of budget-friendly vaporizers. The Slick was released as part of Flowermate's expanding product line, positioned as an affordable, entry-level portable unit. Based on available community references, the device features convection heating, a carbon fiber body, and a removable 18650 battery. It was sold through various authorized retailers, including the well-known online vendor Puff It Up.

The Flowermate Slick occupied the budget segment of the portable vaporizer market, typically retailing at a low price point and frequently appearing on the secondary market within the vaporizer community at prices ranging from free (shipping only) to approximately $40–$55, often with accessories included.

Tips & Techniques

No specific usage tips, loading techniques, or draw methods were discussed in the available community threads for the Flowermate Slick. As a general guideline for convection-style portable vaporizers in this class:

  • Grind: A medium to medium-fine grind is typically recommended for convection devices to allow airflow through the herb while maximizing surface area exposure to heated air.
  • Packing: Convection vaporizers generally perform best with a loose to moderate pack — avoid overpacking, which can restrict airflow and reduce vapor production.
  • Draw technique: Slow, steady draws tend to work best with convection portables, allowing the heated air to fully pass through the herb material.
  • Stir between draws: Some convection portables benefit from stirring the chamber partway through a session to ensure even extraction.

These are general convection vaporizer practices and may not reflect Slick-specific community wisdom, which was not documented in the available threads.

Maintenance & Cleaning

No specific cleaning procedures for the Flowermate Slick were discussed in the available community threads. One marketplace listing noted the device had been "freshly cleaned" before sale, indicating that standard cleaning practices apply. General maintenance guidelines for devices of this type include:

  • Glass mouthpiece: Soak in isopropyl alcohol (90% or higher) periodically to remove residue buildup. Rinse thoroughly with warm water and allow to dry before use.
  • Vapor path: Use isopropyl alcohol and pipe cleaners or cotton swabs to clean the vapor path.
  • Chamber: Brush out ABV (already been vaped material) after each session using the included cleaning brush or a small paintbrush. Periodically swab the chamber with an isopropyl-dampened cotton swab.
  • Bubbler attachment: Soak in isopropyl alcohol to dissolve resin. Rinse thoroughly.
  • Battery contacts: Wipe with a dry cloth periodically to ensure good electrical contact.
