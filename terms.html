Terms & Credits

How this site was built, where the content comes from, and how you can use it.

Content Sources

The Compendium is derived from a community forum that operated for over fifteen years as one of the premier online destinations for dry herb vaporizer enthusiasts. The forum went offline, but a large portion of its content was preserved by the Internet Archive's Wayback Machine.

We retrieved, processed, and synthesized that archived content using AI to produce the wiki-style articles you see here. The goal is to preserve the accumulated knowledge of thousands of enthusiasts — tips, techniques, temperature guides, maintenance advice, and real-world experience — in a form that remains accessible to the community going forward.

AI-Assisted Synthesis

Compendium articles were generated using Claude (Anthropic) as a synthesis tool, not a source of information. Claude was given the original forum posts as input and asked to organize and summarize the community's knowledge. The knowledge itself comes from the community; Claude organized it.

We make no claim that AI-generated summaries are perfectly accurate. If you find an error or disagree with a summary, use the "Suggest an edit" link at the bottom of any article.

Age Restriction

This site covers dry herb vaporizers and cannabis culture. Access is restricted to users who are 21 years of age or older, or the legal age in their jurisdiction — whichever is greater. By using this site you confirm you meet that requirement.

The Round Table is an independent community project. Product coverage in the Compendium reflects community discussion, not commercial relationships. Sponsored content (where it exists) is clearly labeled. Editorial content is not influenced by sponsors or advertisers.
