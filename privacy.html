Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who We Are

The Round Table (theroundtable.wiki) is an independent, community-driven publication covering dry herb vaporizers. It is operated by Muddy River Workshop. Questions about this policy can be directed to guardiansoftheround@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal data and only what is necessary to operate the site and newsletter:

  • Email address — collected only if you subscribe to our newsletter via Beehiiv.
  • Usage data — pages visited, time on site, and general location (country/region) collected anonymously via Google Analytics (GA4). No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.
  • Age verification — a cookie is set when you confirm you are 21 or older. This cookie contains no personal information and expires after 30 days.

We do not collect names, addresses, payment information, or any other personal data beyond the above.

Newsletter (Beehiiv)

Our newsletter is managed by Beehiiv. When you subscribe, your email address is stored on Beehiiv's servers and subject to Beehiiv's privacy policy. We use your email solely to send The Round Table newsletter. We will never sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties.

You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any newsletter email. Your data will be removed from our list promptly upon request.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use the site — which pages are popular, where traffic comes from, and how long people stay. This data is aggregated and anonymous. Google Analytics uses cookies to track sessions.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on or by using a browser with tracking protection enabled.

Cookies

We use the following cookies:

  • rtw_age_verified — set when you confirm you are 21+. Expires in 30 days. Contains no personal data.
  • Google Analytics cookies (_ga, _gid, etc.) — used to distinguish users and sessions for analytics purposes.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the age verification gate — you will be asked to verify your age on each visit.

Third-Party Links

Our site links to external websites including manufacturer sites, retailer pages, and community resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies.

Children's Privacy

This site is intended for users 21 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone under 21. If you believe a minor has submitted information to us, contact us immediately and we will remove it.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other privacy laws, including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at guardiansoftheround@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Privacy questions or data requests: guardiansoftheround@gmail.com
